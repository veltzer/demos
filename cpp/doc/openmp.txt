This is a list of hints for openmp on linux

get info about gcc support for openmp via:
	info libgomp-4.6

the gcc openmp site is at:
	http://gcc.gnu.org/wiki/openmp

the gcc internal list of pragmas is at:
	http://gcc.gnu.org/onlinedocs/gccint/OpenMP.html

the openmp site is at:
	http://openmp.org/wp/

wikipedia openmp definition:
	http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenMP

online documentation for libgomp:
	http://gcc.gnu.org/onlinedocs/libgomp/

the gomp website:
	http://gcc.gnu.org/projects/gomp/
